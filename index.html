About Me

A little bit about who I am

I'm a senior at Eaglecrest High School in Centennial, CO, and I'll be heading to CSU this fall for mechanical engineering. Most of what I enjoy doing comes down to figuring out how things actually work—then trying to build them better. Robotics has been a big part of that. Over the past few years, I've led the electrical side of our robot, wiring everything, planning layouts, and making sure it all holds up in competition. More than anything, I like working on projects where small decisions actually change how the whole thing performs. Outside of that, I've been solving Rubik's cubes since I was a kid and now compete in World Cube Association events across 10+ categories. I like it for the same reason I like engineering—there's always a more efficient way to do something, and once it clicks, it just works.
